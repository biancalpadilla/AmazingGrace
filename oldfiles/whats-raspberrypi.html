What is a Raspberry Pi?

The Raspberry Pi is a low cost, credit-card sized computer that plugs into a computer monitor or TV, and uses a standard keyboard and mouse.

It’s capable of doing everything you’d expect a desktop computer to do, from browsing the internet and playing high-definition video, to making spreadsheets, word-processing, and playing games.

The Raspberry Pi has the ability to interact with the outside world, and has been used in a wide array of projects, from music machines and parent detectors to weather stations and tweeting birdhouses with infra-red cameras.
